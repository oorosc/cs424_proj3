How to use the Parser

To begin with, the parser contains four query features:

1) Scope - The set amount of time we are viewing (All episodes, season x, episode y)
2) Filters - The filters we wish to see, meaning characters, writers, directors, etc. that appeared over the scope
3) Min/Max slider - The max / min % appearances required to be listed
4) And/Or comparison - The sum of your filters with an AND or an OR operator enacted upon them over their scope

To enter a brod category, select the scope or filter, and then click on the button above the respective dropdown. Your selection will automatically be rendered as you choose it. If you wish to add additional filters, simply click the '+' button to the right of the filter dropdown and it shall be saved until you reset the scope, at which point all your filters will be cleared. Sort alphabetical changes the order of the dropdowns, so that they're no longer listed by their numerical/parsed order, but rather alphabetically. If you wish to see the results of your parses real time, switch the view away from the character/season explorer and onto the charts and auto generated table
