Interesting Findings

About the Characters

  • When trying to parse/mine the data, we found that Homer's data wasn't parsed because of the way his entry was recorded. When trying to displaying the family's appearances throughout the seasons we found that Homer wasn't recorded. Dylan confirmed that it was because the wiki listed Homer as appearing in all episodes that the parser skip him.
  • Based on our data, we found that Marge Simpson doesn't have a single appearance throughout Season 24.
  • Throughout the majority of the seasons, Marge Simpson always appears in 1 episode less than Bart and Lisa. This trend holds for Season 2 to Season 19 and Season 25.
  • Bart and Lisa have appeared together in the same episodes throughout the 25 Seasons.
  • Homer, Bart and Lisa have appeared together in the same episodes for 21 out of 25 Seasons. This holds true for Season 1 to Season 4, then from Season 6 to Season 21 and finally Season 24.
  • Homer, Marge, Bart and Lisa hold the spot for the Top 4 Most Appearances.
  • Maggie seems to have first appeared during Season 11 and then disappeared until Season 18.
  • The 5th most shown character was Barney Gumble, who is Homer's "buddy" at Moe's Cavern.

About the Voice Actors

  • The Top 5 Voice Actors by the amount of Roles played are as followed:
    • 1. Hank Azaria
    • 2. Dan Castellaneta
    • 3. Tress MacNeille
    • 4. Harry Shearer
    • 5. Unknown
  • Hank Azaria played about 41% more roles than Dan.
  • After that Dan Castellaneta played 17% more roles than Tress, Tress played about 4% more than Harry and Harry played 22% more than Unknown. After this, Unknown has played about 72% more roles than the 6th Voice Actor which is Pamela Hayden.
  • By appearance count, Dan's roles has 7 more appearances than Hank's roles.
  • There was a character on the show call Dan Castellaneta, who was was voiced by Dan Castellaneta himself.
